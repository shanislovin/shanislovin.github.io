Domestic Cats vs. Wild Cats

Genes Vocals Body
Domestic Cat Has more mutation on genes to do with behaviours such as affection, memory and ability to be tamed through stimuli. Ability to purr Tend to be much smaller in size, with the exception of the Maine Coon breed, which can weigh up to 15.9kg depending on diet and genes.
Wild Cat Tend to be more aggressive to be able to survive the wild. Necessity to dominate is more prevalent as big cats often hunt and eat in groups. Mostly roar and unable to purr Much larger range in sizes, from the small Sand Cats weighing up to 3.5kg, to the Liger, weighing up to 725kg.
